Section 1
Who we are

Vitics is an AI-powered decentralised energy intelligence platform operated by Boom Sustainable Labs. We are registered in Nigeria and operate as a public-interest research and market intelligence service focused on Nigeria's decentralised energy transition.

For the purposes of the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Vitics acts as a data controller in respect of any personal data processed through this platform.

Our data protection contact: privacy@vitics.ai

Section 2
What information we collect

Platform visitor data. When you visit vitics.ai, we may collect standard web analytics data including your approximate geographic region, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This data is aggregated and anonymised. We do not collect names, email addresses, or account information from visitors.

Public digital content (anonymised). Vitics analyses publicly available content from digital platforms including YouTube, public forums, and social media to generate energy intelligence. Before any such content is processed or stored, all personally identifiable information — usernames, profile references, and identifying metadata — is removed. Only anonymised thematic signals are retained.

Contact enquiries. If you contact us directly via email, we retain your email address and the content of your message solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

What we do not collect. We never collect: passwords, payment information, government identification numbers, health data, biometric data, children's data, or any data from private or restricted digital spaces.

Section 3
How we use information

Visitor analytics data is used solely to understand platform usage patterns and improve the user experience. It is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for individual profiling.

Anonymised public content signals are processed by AI models to generate aggregated energy intelligence insights — sentiment trends, product mention patterns, geographic demand signals, and SDG alignment indicators. No individual-level data appears in any published insight.

Contact information is used only to respond to your enquiry and is deleted within 90 days of resolution unless you have requested ongoing communication.

Section 4
Legal basis for processing

Under the NDPA 2023 and GDPR, we process data on the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate interests — processing anonymised public content to generate public-interest energy intelligence, where individual privacy rights are not overridden
  • Public interest — generating insights that support Nigeria's energy transition, SDG progress monitoring, and evidence-based policy development
  • Consent — for any direct communication or subscription services where we request explicit consent
  • Legal obligation — where processing is required to comply with applicable law
Section 5
Data storage & retention

Vitics stores aggregated, anonymised intelligence data on secure servers. No personally identifiable information is retained beyond the point of anonymisation in our data pipeline.

Visitor analytics data is retained for a maximum of 13 months before deletion. Contact enquiry data is retained for 90 days after resolution. Anonymised intelligence datasets are retained indefinitely as they contain no personal data.

Data is processed and stored within jurisdictions that provide adequate data protection standards. Any international transfers comply with applicable transfer mechanisms under both NDPA and GDPR.

Section 6
Data sharing & third parties

We do not sell personal data. We never have and never will.

We use the following third-party services in operating the platform: Mapbox (map rendering — governed by Mapbox privacy policy), Anthropic API (AI text processing — anonymised content only, governed by Anthropic usage policies), and standard web hosting services.

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements consistent with NDPA and GDPR requirements. No personally identifiable information is shared with any third party.

We may share aggregated, non-identifiable intelligence insights with research partners, development finance institutions, and policy bodies in furtherance of our public interest mission. Such sharing contains no personal data.

Section 7
Your rights

Under the NDPA 2023 and GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of access
Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
Right to rectification
Request correction of inaccurate personal data
Right to erasure
Request deletion of your personal data where lawfully applicable
Right to restriction
Request we limit processing of your personal data
Right to object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests
Right to portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vitics.ai. We will respond within 30 days as required by law. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng or your local supervisory authority under GDPR.

Section 8
Cookies & analytics

Vitics uses minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand platform usage. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party behavioural analytics. Any analytics we use are configured for anonymisation and data minimisation.

Essential cookies necessary for platform operation may be set. No consent is required for strictly necessary cookies. Where non-essential cookies are used, we will request your consent before setting them.
